Welcome to the Wawa app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Our app collects and processes the following information:

1.1 User Account Information

  • User ID (automatically generated)
  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored as a hash)
  • Account creation date
  • Last update date
  • Login attempts and account lock status

1.2 Profile Information

  • First name and last name
  • Full name (as displayed)
  • Biography
  • Location
  • Profile picture (avatar)
  • Profile tags
  • Social media accounts

1.3 Skill Profiles

  • Certification information (link, issuing school, issuing body)
  • Years of experience
  • Professional title
  • Verification status

1.4 Resort Profiles (for resort staff)

  • Role at the resort
  • Join date
  • Active status

1.5 Activity and Proposal Information

  • Activity titles and descriptions
  • Start and end dates
  • Session details
  • Associated skill profiles and tags
  • Creator information
  • Approval status and approver information

1.6 Resort Information

  • Resort name, email, and contact details
  • Location information (address, country, city, coordinates)
  • Social media and website URLs
  • Description and amenities
  • Verification status

1.7 Media Content

  • Profile pictures
  • Activity images
  • Resort thumbnails and gallery images
  • Certification documents

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Build and maintain your user profile
  • Verify and showcase your skills and certifications
  • Facilitate activity proposals and bookings
  • Connect travelers with resorts and instructors
  • Manage resort profiles and verify their information
  • Personalize your app experience
  • Ensure the safety and security of our community
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With resorts and instructors when you make or accept a booking
  • With other users when you create or participate in activities
  • With Wawa administrators for verification and approval processes
  • With service providers who assist in our operations
  • For legal requirements or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Storing passwords as hashes
  • Implementing account locking mechanisms to prevent unauthorized access
  • Secure storage of files and media content

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your account and profile information
  • Manage your skill profiles and certifications
  • Control your privacy settings and what information is visible to others
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection and use

You can manage many of these options directly in your account settings or by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
